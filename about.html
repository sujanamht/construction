Who Are We?


Niraula Engineering Construction started its journey on 2015. Over the years, we have experienced significant growth and success, establishing ourselves as a dynamic construction firm and a prominent player in the construction industry.

Our success is attributed to a strategic blend of experience and youthful energy within our team. Seasoned industry veterans bring a wealth of knowledge, ensuring a solid foundation of expertise, while our younger talents infuse the company with fresh ideas, innovation, and a forward-thinking approach.

Since inception we have successfully undertaken and completed a diverse range of projects. Having successfully handed over multiple buildings, roads, water supply, earthquake reconstruction and many other projects, our commitment to quality and client satisfaction remains unwavering. Our growth is not just quantitative but qualitative, reflecting our Management, Construction practices.

As we move forward, we are excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. With a passionate and skilled team, we are confident in our ability to continue delivering excellence in construction projects, contributing to the growth and development of the communities we serve.
